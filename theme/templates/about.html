About Negosyo Center Antipolo

Negosyo Center Antipolo is one of the satelite offices of the Department of Trade and Industry - Rizal, that caters to the needs of aspiring entrepreneurs and existing micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) in Antipolo City.

As part of the Negosyo Center program of the DTI-Rizal Provincial Office, in partnership with Antipolo City Government, NC-Antipolo provides various services and resources to help clients start, sustain, and grow their businesses.

Services offered include business registration assistance, business advisory and counseling, market information, business opportunity matching, access to finance, and capability-building programs such as seminars, training, and workshops.

The highly trained and experienced business counselors are committed to providing quality and responsive services to clients. NC-Antipolo also collaborates with government agencies, non-government organizations, and private sector partners to provide clients with more comprehensive and effective support.

Negosyo Center Antipolo believes that empowering entrepreneurs can contribute to the economic growth and development of the city and the country. NC-Antipolo continuously improves its programs and services to ensure that it meets the changing needs of clients and the business environment.
